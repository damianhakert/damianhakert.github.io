Accessing the Office

  • Enter the building through the double doors between the two Mike's Bikes locations.
  • Find 'GitLab' in the intercom directory next to the door to have us buzz you in.
  • Proceed to the elevator at the rear of the foyer and go to the 2nd floor.
  • Exit the elevator and procees to the right. The unit is the last door on the right at the end of the hall (2F).

A not so typical day at the office

Here at GitLab, we work remotely, but we all have the option of working in the office. Most of the time the office has only a few people, but recently a whole crew of us were in San Francisco on the same day for work.
