VISIT OUR HEADQUARTERS

While our employees work remotely most of the time, we have a beautiful office in the heart of San Francisco. We'd love to have you visit!

Our Office Location

1233 Howard St 2F
San Francisco, CA 94103
United States of America

Above Mike's Bikes
Two blocks from Civic Center BART
Four blocks from 7th street exit from I-80 E / US-101 N

Accessing the Office

  • Enter the building through the double doors between the two Mike's Bikes locations.
  • On the intercom press the call button then press '200' (for GitLab) to have us buzz you in. Note to team members: press 0 to open the door from our cordless phones.
  • Proceed to the elevator at the rear of the foyer and go to the 2nd floor (strangely the elevator will show 3 when you stop at 2).
  • Exit the elevator and proceed to the right. The unit is the last door on the right at the end of the hall (2F).

A not so typical day at the office

Here at GitLab, we work remotely, but we all have the option of working in the office. Most of the time, only a few people are at the office, but on this day, we all coordinated to come into the office together.
