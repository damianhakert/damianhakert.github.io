Feature-packed

Future-Proof: Browse your code, review its history and work together on its future. Mention your colleagues anywhere and they get updated automatically.

The Right Tools: GitLab is an advanced Git-repository manager. It introduces a powerful code review and issue-tracking system, complete with GitLab CI: a powerful continuous integration tool.

Integrated and Ready: GitLab has integrations for tons of tools such as Hipchat, Slack, LDAP, JIRA and more.

Scalable and easily-managed

Scales Effortlessly: Running smoothly on tiny servers and scaling easily to large clusters of many machines, a single server handles thousands of users and repositories.
