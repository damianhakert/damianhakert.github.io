Training

We organize training workshops to help your organization implement Git & GitLab quickly.

End user training workshops focus on Git concepts and the GitLab workflow.

A Git workshop is 3.5 hours and covers git concepts such as committing, branches, merge requests, merge conflicts, tags, cherry-picking, bisecting and rebasing.

A GitLab flow workshop help your organization develop a good git workflow in 2 hours

We can also do a GitLab administrator workshops of 3 hours.

And we're open to discussing other materials to cover and other durations.
