GitLab as a Service

GitLab.com will host your software projects for free.

Join Our Community!

  • It offers private repositories, issue-tracking, wikis and continuous integration.

  • It runs GitLab Enterprise Edition and GitLab CI.

  • No installation required, just sign up below for a free account!

Advantages over GitHub

  • Unlimited repositories
  • Unlimited private collaborators
  • Unlimited disk space*
  • Completely free, no credit card required
  • Free Continuous Integration (CI) on ci.gitlab.com

    • * For performance reasons, there is a soft limit of 1GB per repository

Support package

  • Free subscribers can use the GitLab.com Support Forum if they have questions.
  • If you purchase GitLab.com Bronze Support you can email support directly for timely, personal and private answers. This costs $9.99 per user per year for next-business-day response time and is available in packs of 20 users.

Availability/Security

  • GitLab.com is monitored 24/7. For updates follow @gitlabstatus or see our status page.
