Head of sales vacancy

Gitlab is quickly becoming more popular amongst organisations throughout the world to manage their source code. To keep up with our growth Gitlab.com is looking for a head of sales.

A head of sales would need to have B2B opensource sales experience. Experience selling RedHat or MySQL in the U.S. would be ideal. You would need to have closed large deals ($250k+) before. As a head of sales you would obviously need to articulate sales strategy and tactics and lead our account managers. You have operate with respect, tenacity and compassion in discussions about the open source nature of GitLab.

Compensation could be in the form of a large performance based bonus or in the form of a company stake.

Interested in joining our motivated team? Send us a short motivation plus your resume to sytse at gitlab dot com
